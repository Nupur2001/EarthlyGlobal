                <h2><Strong>1. Basic Multani Mitti Mask</Strong></h2>

                <h3><strong>Ingredients:</strong></h3>
                <p>
                    2 tablespoons of multani mitti<br>
                    Rose water or plain water (enough to make a paste)<br>

                <h3><strong>Instructions:</strong></h3>
                <p>
